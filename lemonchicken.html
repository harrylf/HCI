Lemon Herbed Chicken

Time: 25 minuntes

Tools needed: Pan, knife, baking sheet.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • Lemon
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • All spice
  • Basil
  • Garic powder
  • Lemon pepper

Procedure:

  1. Lay chicken on plate and season both sides with all spice, basil, garlic powder, and lemon pepper
  2. Cut lemon in half. Squeeze juice from one half on chicken
  3. Warm up olive oil in pan, place garlic cloves in oil. Infuse juice of other half of lemon into oil
  4. Sear chicken 2 minutes each side or until a layer of white about 1/8 inch forms
  5. Place chicken on baking sheet and squeeze remaining lemon juice and place lemon halves on sheet
  6. Bake chicken in oven at 350 F for 18 minutes or until white throughout

Recommended side: Pasta
