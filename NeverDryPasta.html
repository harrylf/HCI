Never Dry Pasta

Tools needed: Measuring cup, strainer

Ingredients:

  • Boiled noodles
  • Excess boiled water

Procedure:

  1. Save 2-3 cups of the strained water after the noodles have boiled
  2. After the noodles have been strained, do not let them dry out!
  3. Ocassionally dump the excess boiled water over the strainer full of the noodles every 2-4 minutes before serving the meal

The excess water is full of starch and pasta flavor post boil. Using this hot water will ensure the noodles fresh before serving the meal!
