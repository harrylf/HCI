Lemon Infusion

Time: 5 minute prep

Tools needed: Knife, pitcher, cutting board

Ingredients:

  • 2 Lemons
  • Water
  • Ice

Procedure:

  1. Thinly slice both lemons
  2. Place sliced lemons into pitcher
  3. Add two quarts of water to pitcher
  4. Refrigerate 2 to 4 hours to allow lemons to infuse
  5. Stir well and strain, discarding the lemons
  6. Serve with plenty of ice

Give your tastebuds a hydration switch-up!
