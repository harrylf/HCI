Sesame Soy Salmon

Time: 25 minuntes

Tools needed: Pot, baking sheet, parchment paper, glazing brush.

Ingredients:

  • Salmon fillets
  • 1/4 cup sweet and sour sauce
  • 1/8 cup soy sauce
  • Sesame seeds
  • All spice
  • Cinnamon
  • Chili powder
  • Paprika

Procedure:

Salmon

  1. Lay salmon on plate and season sides with all spice, cinnamon, chili powder, and paprika
  2. To prevent salmon from getting dry, run paper towel under luke warm water and wring out. Gently lay damp paper towel on salmon and remove.
  3. Bake salmon at 400 F on parchment lined baking sheet for 17 minutes
  4. Glaze salmon with sesame sauce

Sesame Sauce

  1. Place sweet and sour sauce, soy sauce, and sesame seed in pot
  2. Gently heat on lowest heat and stir until combined

Recommended side: Udon
