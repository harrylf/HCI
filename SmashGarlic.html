Smash Garlic

Time: 5 minute prep

Tools needed: Knife, cutting board

Ingredients:

  • 2 garlic cloves

Procedure:

  1. Use the knife to trim off the tip of the root
  2. Put garlic clove on cutting board
  3. Place flat side of knife on top of the clove, with the blade facing away from you
  4. Use your hand to press the flat side of the knife against the clove
  5. Use a rocking motion to thoroughly crush
  6. Peel the skin, the rocking motion should have loosened the clove
  7. You may now either smash it further, chop it, or mince it before cooking
